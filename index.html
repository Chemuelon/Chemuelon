Reflection: The Gap Between Identity and Reputation

The most valuable insight from this semester's persona work is recognizing that alignment between who I am and how I'm perceived is a continuous practice, not a destination. While my identity as an innovative, resilient entrepreneur largely matches my reputation as an expressive, proactive leader, the feedback loop has revealed crucial growth edges.

This evolution will make me not just a better leader, but a more effective collaborator and team builder.
